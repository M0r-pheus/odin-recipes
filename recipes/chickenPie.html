Creamy Chicken Pie Recipe

Description

Creamy Chicken Pie is a delightful and comforting dish that combines tender chunks of succulent chicken with a rich and velvety cream sauce, all encased in a golden, flaky pastry. This classic recipe is a savory indulgence that captures the essence of home-cooked goodness. The filling boasts a luscious blend of cooked chicken pieces, gently simmered in a creamy sauce made with a harmonious mix of aromatic herbs, spices, and a generous helping of smooth cream. The result is a flavorful and hearty concoction that satisfies both the palate and the soul.

The buttery, golden-brown pastry crust adds the perfect finishing touch to this culinary masterpiece, providing a satisfying crunch with every bite. As the creamy chicken filling melds harmoniously with the flaky crust, each forkful delivers a burst of comforting flavors that evoke a sense of warmth and nostalgia. Creamy Chicken Pie is a timeless classic, perfect for cozy family dinners or gatherings with friends, bringing people together with its irresistible combination of creamy goodness and savory elegance.

Ingredients

For the Filling
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
For the Pastry
  • 1 package store-bought puff pastry sheets (or homemade if preferred)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add diced onions and minced garlic, sauté until softened
  3. Sprinkle flour over the sautéed onions and garlic, stirring constantly to create a roux
  4. Gradually whisk in the chicken broth and heavy cream, ensuring a smooth consistency. Cook until the mixture thickens
  5. Add diced chicken, mixed vegetables, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined. Simmer for a few minutes until the filling is heated through
  6. Roll out the puff pastry sheets on a floured surface. Line a pie dish with one sheet, leaving excess hanging over the edges.
  7. Pour the creamy chicken filling into the pastry-lined dish. Cover with the second puff pastry sheet, sealing the edges by crimping with a fork
  8. Brush the top of the pastry with the beaten egg to achieve a golden finish
  9. Cut a few slits on the top pastry to allow steam to escape during baking
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the pastry is golden brown and the filling is bubbly
  11. Allow the Creamy Chicken Pie to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving

    Enjoy your delicious Creamy Chicken Pie!
